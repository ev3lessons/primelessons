About Us




PrimeLessons.org was founded in January 2020 to support users of LEGO Education SPIKE Prime. The founders have a long history of creating and sharing educational curriculum and resources. Their goal is to provide open-source, high-quality lessons to students, teachers and robotics teams everywhere. SPIKE Prime Lessons is affiliated with EV3Lessons.com which is the premier site for learning to program the LEGO MINDSTORMS EV3.

Everything shared on PrimeLessons is provided free of charge. If you wish to support the site in any way, please email (team@ev3lessons.com).
